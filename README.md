# Pyinecraft 2.0

Pyinecraft is a simple minecraft clone made with Python.

## Bugs

1. Sometimes you will be stuck (You cannot move or jump)
   You will need to break a block.
   
## How To Play:

1 = Grass <br>
2 = Dirt <br>
3 = Stone <br>
4 = Cobblestone <br>
5 = Sand <br>
6 = Log <br>
7 = Planks <br>
8 = Obsidian <br>
9 = Ice <br>
